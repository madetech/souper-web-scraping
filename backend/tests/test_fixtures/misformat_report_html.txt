<h2 id="outcome-of-service-assessment">Outcome of service assessment</h2>

<p>The Car tax, <abbr title="Car tax, Statutory Off Road Notification">SORN</abbr> &amp; Vehicle Enquiry service was seeking permission to continue development and move to Public Beta.</p>

<p>After reviewing the service <abbr title="Government Digital Services">GDS</abbr> have concluded the Car tax, <abbr title="Car tax, Statutory Off Road Notification">SORN</abbr> &amp; Vehicle Enquiry service has shown sufficient progress and evidence of meeting the pre-April 2014 criteria and should proceed to Public Beta.</p>

<p><abbr title="Government Digital Services">GDS</abbr> were very impressed by the pace at which the new service has been delivered and the capacity to iterate quickly.</p>

<h2 id="about-the-service">About the service</h2>

<p>Users will be able to apply for a new tax disc, take their vehicle off the road by declaring Statutory Off Road Notification, or use the vehicle enquiry service to check what information <abbr title="Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency">DVLA</abbr> holds on its database about a vehicle.</p>

<h2 id="reasons">Reasons</h2>

<ol>
  <li>
    <p>The new service is a significant improvement on the legacy service and has focused on meeting user needs, acting on customer feedback and usability issues associated with the legacy service.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>The service can be iteratively improved at pace and the existing team will continue to develop the service, with plans to iterate based on analysis of data following beta launch.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>The service has the appropriate security and privacy protections in place.</p>
  </li>
</ol>

<h2 id="recommendations">Recommendations</h2>

<p>To be addressed as soon as possible after the service has passed to public beta and a condition of making the beta the default option on the <a rel="external" href="http://gov.uk/">GOV.UK</a> service start pages.</p>

<ol>
  <li>
    <p>Design and content snag list feedback to be actioned to ensure departures from the style guide are addressed.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>The team should establish a regular series of face-to-face user research, integrated into sprint cycles.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>The team should carry out testing of the beta service with people with a range of disabilities and consult the <abbr title="Government Digital Services">GDS</abbr> accessibility team for recommendations.</p>
  </li>
</ol>

<h2 id="other-recommendations">Other Recommendations</h2>

<ol>
  <li>
    <p>Design resource should be brought within team to ensure someone owns the design and design changes (as a result of feedback/data analysis) can be delivered quickly.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Implement plan to separate secret code separate from platform and publish non-sensitive code as open source on GitHub or a similar public repository.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>It is not currently possible to bookmark or link to the results page (state maintained via secret code passed back and forth in page/form). This should be addressed to enable a change in state to show a new URL.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Fix the style 404 page.</p>
  </li>
</ol>

<h2 id="next-steps">Next Steps</h2>

<p>This service has been given approval to launch as a Public Beta service.</p>

<h2 id="summary">Summary</h2>

<p>In summary, <abbr title="Government Digital Services">GDS</abbr> are very pleased to report that this service is on track to meet the service standard. <abbr title="Government Digital Services">GDS</abbr> are very pleased to be adding a digital service to <a rel="external" href="http://gov.uk/">GOV.UK</a> that is on track to demonstrate it is so good users will prefer to use it.</p>
